About Me

Director, Legal, Compliance & Risk | Adekunle Ajiboye

A seasoned legal and compliance professional with over 18 years of progressive experience spanning legal practice, commercial banking, and international consulting. I hold a Master of Laws (LL.M) from the University of Ibadan, a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) from Lagos State University, and am a Barrister & Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria.

I am a Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS) and hold an International Diploma in Compliance from the University of Manchester. My career has taken me through leading institutions including First Bank of Nigeria, Sterling Bank, Standard Chartered Bank Nigeria (as Country Head of Compliance and Consumer Banking Legal & Compliance), and Ecobank Transnational Inc. in Lomé, Togo (as Senior Group Officer, Compliance).

Currently serving as Director of Legal, Compliance & Risk at SimpliAlpha Consulting Limited, I design and implement governance frameworks, regulatory compliance programs, and enterprise risk strategies. I have led AML/CFT training initiatives, advised C-suite executives and boards on regulatory engagement, and built robust compliance cultures across multiple jurisdictions in Sub-Saharan Africa.
